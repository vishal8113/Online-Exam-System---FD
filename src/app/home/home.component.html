How It Works

1. Register / Login

Create an account or log in to access exams and reports.

2. Take Timed Exams

Attempt level-wise tests with increasing difficulty.

3. Get Instant Reports

View results immediately and progress to the next level.

Adaptive Exam Levels

Level 1

Start here. Meet the passing criteria to unlock Level 2.

Level 2

Increased difficulty. Pass to access Level 3.

Level 3

Advanced level with final report generation.
